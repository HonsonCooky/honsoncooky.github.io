I have a Bachelor of Engineering with Honours (First Class) in Software Engineering from Victoria University of Wellington, with a specialisation in Networked Applications.

Outside of work, I've been spending time with C. Low level programming is really where I enjoy myself - as close to the bits as possible, ultimately chasing game development at its most raw level.
