Harrison Cook

Software Engineer

Born into a family addicted to music and theatre, I spent years on stage before calculus and physics pulled me in a different direction. The result is a wild mix of showman and nerd, engulfed by computers, and having a blast.

These days, I'm working at Splitting Point Studios - building backend services in Go and Rust for massive multiplayer games. I've also had the opportunity to build production systems in C# for one of the largest SaaS platforms in the country, and in TypeScript for New Zealand's largest fuel distributor.

All of which followed on from my Bachelor of Engineering with First Class Honours, majoring in Software Engineering with a specialisation in Networked Applications (quite the mouthful).

Outside of work, my mind wanders to game development in C - low level, from scratch, no engine. There's something deeply satisfying about building worlds from nothing but instructions.
