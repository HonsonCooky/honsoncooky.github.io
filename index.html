Harrison Cook

From stage lights to system calls. I grew up doing theatre and music - a big part of how I got here, even if it is not where I ended up. Somewhere along the way the maths and science side won out, but the combination stuck - part creative, part nerd, fully both.

These days I work at Splitting Point Studios, writing Go APIs for Roblox games at scale. Before that I was at Xero (C#) and Z Energy (TypeScript), building and maintaining production systems. I have a BE(Hons) in Software Engineering from Victoria University of Wellington, where I specialised in networked applications and spent a lot of time thinking about how computers actually work.

That is still what I find most interesting - the layer below the abstraction. I like knowing why something works, not just that it does. Right now that looks like Go. Over time, more C and Zig.
